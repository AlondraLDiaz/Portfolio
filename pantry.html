Project Overview


Pantry is a food guide app that helps people easily explore food recipes and local cuisines to ease the choice in planning a meal or deciding where and what to eat. Pantry was created to help plan meals ahead of time so that one is able to save money and time while providing a healthy meal to serve any family size.This projects addresses help our users create meal plans that fit their schedule so they can achieve a more productive meal time without having to worry about what to cook.

Research


We wanted to understand what were the challenges when it comes to feeding a family. We all had our experiences as to why it could be difficult for our own lives but we kept several questions in mind as we conducted our research by conducting interviews with family households. Through our research we wanted to identify most users current meal planning habits. Understand users typical food preferences and tastes. Discover user pain points of feeding a family on a weekly basis.

Definition & Syntheis


As we defined our problem through our survey we were ready to further develop our questions for our interviews based on our survey results. We synthesized the data from our survey and interviews to help use start identfying how we can resolve the challenges facing our users goal's when it comes to meal planning. We deduced from our data the solutions needed for an app and began ideating a design that is friendly, organized, and fun for our users to spend time, money and energy out of meal planning onto their plates.

Ideation


We ideated using an Affinity Diagram and Feature Priotization Matrix. These highlighted what we could feasibly include in our app while also providing a resolution to the user. As we gathered our top features, we began sketching our wireframes and created a low-fidelity protoype.

Low Fidelity


To test the functionality of our app, we conducted usablity testing early into our protoype to ensure we could refine our app and iterate our final prototype to be functional and intutive to our users.
